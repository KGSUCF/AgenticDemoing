How to add the photo to Gertrude Shell
=======================================

Place the Gertrude & Marty portrait photo in this directory (photos/)
with this filename:

    gertrude_and_marty.jpg

(The beautiful WWII-era portrait of Gertrude and Marty together)

The shell will display it prominently in the center of the main board.

Photo requirements:
    - Supported formats: JPEG (.jpg / .jpeg) or PNG (.png)
    - Recommended size: at least 200x250 pixels (displayed at ~260x320)
    - Portrait orientation works beautifully
    - The app requires Pillow to display real photos:
        pip install Pillow

If the photo file is not found (or Pillow is not installed), the app shows
a warm placeholder with "Gertrude & Marty" text instead. The app runs
perfectly fine without the photo — adding it is optional personalisation.

Tip: Copy the file here and rename it to gertrude_and_marty.jpg,
then restart the shell.
