GERTRUDE SHELL - PHOTO INSTRUCTIONS
=====================================

This folder is where Gertrude's photos go.

WHAT TO ADD:
  gertrude.jpg   - A photo of Gertrude (displayed on the main board)
  marty.jpg      - A photo of Marty (displayed on the main board)

RECOMMENDED SIZES:
  - 300x300 pixels or larger (will be displayed at ~200x200 in the UI)
  - JPEG or PNG format
  - Portrait orientation works best

HOW TO ADD THEM:
  1. Copy your photo files into this folder (photos/).
  2. Name them exactly: gertrude.jpg and marty.jpg
  3. Restart the app — the photos will appear automatically on the home board.

If the photo files are missing, the app shows a placeholder silhouette image
so the app still works perfectly without photos.

NOTE: If you want to use a different filename (e.g., gertrude.png), update the
`photoPaths` object in main.js accordingly.
