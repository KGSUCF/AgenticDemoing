GERTRUDE SHELL — PHOTO INSTRUCTIONS
=====================================

Place the Gertrude & Marty portrait photo in this folder (photos/)
with this filename:

    gertrude_and_marty.jpg

(The beautiful WWII-era portrait of Gertrude and Marty together)

RECOMMENDED SIZE:
  - At least 260 × 320 pixels (portrait orientation)
  - JPEG or PNG format

HOW TO ADD:
  1. Copy the photo file into this folder (photos/).
  2. Name it exactly: gertrude_and_marty.jpg
  3. Restart the app — the photo appears automatically on the main board.

If the file is missing the app shows a warm placeholder — it still works
perfectly without the photo. Adding it is optional personalisation.

Also accepted (fallbacks, in order):
  gertrude_and_marty.png
  gertrude.jpg
  gertrude.png
