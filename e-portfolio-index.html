About

Karen Strouse is an independent telecommunications consultant with more than three decades of experience helping providers navigate competitive markets worldwide. Her engagements span marketing strategy, business planning, mergers and acquisitions, executive education, and organizational redesign — with clients ranging from Fortune 25 carriers to emerging-market wireless startups across Latin America, the Middle East, and Asia.

Before founding her independent practice, Karen served as Consulting Manager at Deloitte & Touche (formerly Touche Ross) and held senior roles at Bell Atlantic and Bell of Pennsylvania. She is the author of three industry books published by Artech House, a frequent contributor to leading telecommunications journals, and a recognized speaker at international industry conferences.
